About the Chairman – Engr. Dr. Darlington Ugota

Building Wealth. Nurturing Talent. Inspiring Growth.

From humble beginnings, Engr. Dr. Darlington Ugota built a real estate empire from scratch — driven by vision, discipline, and a commitment to mentorship. His journey has inspired a new generation of Nigerian real estate leaders, many of whom have grown into massively successful business giants, under his guidance and mentorship.

Dr. Ugota is deeply involved in educative initiatives, including the acclaimed Wealth and Wisdom Podcast, which equips young entrepreneurs and investors with insights on real estate, wealth creation, and strategic growth.

Core Values & Vision

● Strategic Excellence: Thoughtful investments and high-growth properties.

● Empowerment: Educating clients and nurturing young talent.

● Community Transformation: Building estates, supporting youth, and social initiatives.
